We are very proud that our project has already been featured in 22 news outlets, both regular press as well as online channels, across 6 European countries.

The success of our project depends crucially on the amount of press coverage that we are able to achieve. We would be extremely grateful for any help that you may be able to provide with publicising further our message.

If you wish to write an article about our project, feel free to download our press-kit, or contact us directly. We will be more than happy to arrange for an interview and answer any questions you may have.
